Our Services

At The Absolute Property Group, we offer a range of services to help you manage your property portfolio. We are a team of experienced professionals who are dedicated to providing the best possible service to our clients.

Executive Management Agents

As Executive Managing Agents (EMA) by CSOS we provide trusted, professional leadership to restore stability and effective governance, acting in the best interests of all owners and residents.

Maintenance

We liaise on behalf of the Maintenance Trustee with appointed contractors to manage all maintenance and repairs of the common property efficiently.

Commercial Property Management

Our Commercial Property Management services are designed to streamline the rental process, ensuring smooth transactions and a positive experience for both landlords and tenants.

Flexy Handy-Man Services

Flexy Handy-Man Services is a comprehensive maintenance and repair service provider that offers a wide range of services to ensure the smooth operation and upkeep of your property.

Meter Reading

Our meter reading services are designed to ensure accurate and timely billing of utility services. We are a team of experienced professionals who are dedicated to providing the best possible service to our clients.
